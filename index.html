BENEFITS OF REGULAR EXERCISE

Exercise is defined as any physical activity that makes your muscles work and requires your body to burn calories. Living an active way of life also offers a plethora of health benefits, which in turn have a positive impact on both the mental and physical well-being; indeed, it could make you live longer.

  • Improved Physical Health

    Regular exercise strengthens the cardiovascular system, improves muscle tone, and enhances overall physical fitness. It also helps in maintaining a healthy weight and can prevent or manage chronic diseases such as heart disease, diabetes, and obesity.

  • Better Mental Health and Reduced Stress

    Physical activity stimulates the production of endorphins, which are natural mood lifters. Regular exercise can also reduce symptoms of anxiety and depression, and improve overall mental well-being.

  • Boosted Energy Levels and Stamina

    Regular physical activity improves cardiovascular health and increases stamina. Over time, this leads to higher energy levels and improved overall performance in daily activities.

What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measurement that uses your height and weight to estimate your body fat. It is a simple and widely used method for assessing whether you are in a healthy weight range. Although BMI does not directly measure body fat, it is a useful screening tool to categorize weight status and potential health risks.
